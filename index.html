Naturopathy is a system that uses natural remedies to help the body heal itself. It embraces many therapies, including herbs, acupuncture, exercise, and nutritional counseling. The goal of naturopathic medicine is to treat the whole person -- that means mind, body, and spirit. It also aims to heal the root causes of an illness -- not just stop the symptoms.
